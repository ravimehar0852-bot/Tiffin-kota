Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy a property through U.S.K GROUP?
Simply contact us via phone or WhatsApp at 8000556793. Our property experts will understand your requirements and shortlist the best options across Kota and Rajasthan within your budget.
What types of loans do you assist with?
We assist with home loans, business loans, personal loans, lap (loan against property), and education loans. We work with 20+ banks and NBFCs to get you the best rates.
Do you offer construction services for residential projects?
Yes! We handle complete residential construction from design and planning to interior finishing. We use quality materials and employ experienced engineers and contractors.
Is business consultancy only for large companies?
Not at all. We serve startups, MSMEs, and growing businesses alike. Whether you need GST registration, business planning, or growth strategy — we're here for every stage.
How can digital marketing help my business?
We run targeted campaigns on Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google, and YouTube. Our content team creates engaging posts, reels, and ads that bring real customers — not just followers.
Where is U.S.K GROUP located?
Our main office is at Borkheda, Kota, Rajasthan. You can walk in, or reach us via call/WhatsApp at 8000556793. We serve clients across entire Rajasthan.
